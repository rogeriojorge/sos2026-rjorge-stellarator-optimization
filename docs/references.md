## Scientific reading list

- Landreman, Medasani, Wechsung, Giuliani, Jorge, and Zhu, "SIMSOPT: A flexible framework for stellarator optimization," JOSS 2021.
- Landreman and Paul, optimized quasisymmetric stellarator configurations and related QA/QH design papers.
- Jorge and collaborators on single-stage stellarator optimization.
- NESCOIL, REGCOIL, FOCUS, and SIMSOPT coil-design literature for current potentials and filamentary coils.
- Helander, "Theory of plasma confinement in non-axisymmetric magnetic fields," Reports on Progress in Physics 2014.
- W7-X neoclassical optimization and validation literature by Beidler, Dinklage, and collaborators.
- Modern quasi-isodynamic, quasisymmetric, and power-plant-oriented optimization papers by Goodman, Landreman, Henneberg, Mynick, and collaborators.
- DESC and gyrokinetic/turbulence-optimization work, including DESC-GX and related nonlinear validation studies.
